# Hoverworks Ltd. (Version 2)

# Description
Hoverworks Limited is the world's premier (and so far only) hovercraft retailer. Based out of a quiet suburb in the Pacific Northwest, Hoverworks Limited seeks to enhance the lives of its customers by providing fun, innovative and environmentally-friendly new options for their daily commutes. Every Hoverworks Limited Hovercraft comes prepackaged with our custom V3NT ventilation system that allows our products to soar to new heights without drawing power from anything other than the air you breathe.

To break it down, Hoverworks products use a top-of-the-line ventilation system that allows for a smooth intake of air, pressurization, and expulsion of said air to keep them afloat. No need to pay for gasoline or worry about whether you remembered to charge your vehicle anymore! Be the talk of your cul-de-sac and do your part to save energy today!

# Behind the scenes
This project was created to explore an interest I've had since I was a child: alternative, energy-efficient forms of transportation. Since I was a kid I wanted to see if hoverboards (and hoverbikes) could serve as an effective alternative means of travel, but it always seemed to be constrained to the realm of science fiction. Well, I'm not studying mechanical engineering so creating the hovercrafts themselves is probably outside my wheelhouse, but I'd still love to attempt to design a website that mimics a store's website where users could potentially purchase hoverboards nonetheless.
