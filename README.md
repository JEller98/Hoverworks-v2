Hoverworks Ltd.

Hoverworks Limited is the world's premier (and so far only) hovercraft retailer. Based out of the Pacific Northwest, located in a rural town on the West Coast of the United States, Hoverworks Limited seeks to enhance the lives of its customers by providing fun, innovative and environmentally-friendly new options for their daily commutes. Every Hoverworks Limited Hovercraft comes prepackaged with our custom V3NT ventilation system that allows our products to soar to new heights without drawing power from anything other than the air you breathe.

To break it down, Hoverworks products use a top-of-the-line ventilation system that allows for a smooth intake of air, pressurization, and expulsion of said air to keep them afloat.
